The Problem

The e-commerce fashion industry faces a $600 billion crisis: high return rates driven by inconsistent sizing. Consumers want personalization powered by data.

Our AI Solution

We employ Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to analyze photos and generate a precise 3D measurement map for an accurate fit.

Strategic Impact

AuraStyle AI is a B2B SaaS integration. We project a 25% reduction in return rates, fostering a sustainable fashion ecosystem.

Ethical Considerations

Privacy is priority. Data is processed via Edge Computing; photos never leave the user's device. We audit for Fit Fairness and inclusivity.
